What we do..,

   Trapo Tour is a destination discovery website that helps travellers find the right destinations according to their preferences.
      At Trapo Tour, we aim to make discovering your next holiday destination as much of an eye-opener as your vacation itself.
         We help you break out of the monotony of the same vacation spots you have known for years and open in front of you, a world of
            possibilities.With our interactive map interface, you can plan your vacation like never before.

Travel freely. Travel to places you haven't seen. Enjoy your life. Find Sri Lanka's Aesthetics. Join us to find out about your unseen destinations.
